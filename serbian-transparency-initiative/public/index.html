What We Cover

Two Fronts. One Mission.

We currently accept reports in two focus areas where corruption has gone unchecked for too long.

Reports of financial misconduct, property misuse, abuse of power, or fraud involving Serbian Orthodox institutions and affiliated organizations.

About Us

An independent voice for those who don’t have one.

The Serbian Transparency Initiative (STI) is a U.S.-based organization dedicated to exposing corruption and protecting the people it affects most: everyday members of the Serbian diaspora community.

We started with a single mission: give people a safe channel to speak up when institutions fail them. Whether the abuse happens inside a parish office or inside a trucking company, the person reporting it deserves protection, respect, and a real chance at justice.

We are independent. We are confidential. We are on the side of the people who speak up.

Independent No political or institutional ties
Confidential Your identity is never shared without consent
Accountable Every report is reviewed and tracked
Trucking Industry

Truck drivers deserve fairness, not exploitation.

Serbian Transparency Initiative is now accepting anonymous reports related to abuse and fraud in the trucking industry.

If you have experienced any of the following, we want to hear from you:

  • Unpaid wages or missing miles
  • Fake or manipulated rate confirmations
  • Pressure to violate logbook regulations
  • Illegal deductions from your paycheck
  • Misleading or deceptive load agreements

All reports are confidential. You do not need to provide your name. Evidence like pay stubs, rate confirmations, messages, and logs helps us investigate, but is never required.

How It Works

Three simple steps. Zero exposure.

01

Submit Your Report

Fill out the secure form with as much or as little detail as you can share. You can remain completely anonymous.

02

We Review & Verify

Our team reviews every submission, cross-references evidence where possible, and assesses the credibility and urgency of each case.

03

We Take Action

Verified cases are escalated to media, legal partners, or relevant authorities in a way that always protects the reporter’s identity.

Church & Religious Institutions

Faith deserves integrity.

Trust placed in religious institutions is sacred, and when that trust is violated, the damage runs deep.

We accept reports of financial misconduct, property misuse, abuse of authority, and concealment of wrongdoing within Serbian Orthodox institutions and affiliated organizations.

Every report is handled with discretion. Your identity is protected. We never contact the institution you’re reporting without your permission.
